Rules & Guidelines

Start of Shift Protocol
  • The first 1.5 to 2 hours of your shift is dedicated to:
    • Reviewing your daily agenda
    • Processing all customer emails
    • Responding to SMS communications
  • Prioritize urgent customer messages flagged with High Priority tags
Personal Time Management
  • 15 minutes total allocated for personal time per shift
  • Track usage in SYCO Time Tracker
  • If exceeding limit:
    • Immediately notify your Tier 2/3 supervisor
    • Document reason in shift log
Floor Management
  • When leaving the floor:
    • Notify at least one Tier 2/3 supervisor
    • Update your status in Teams
    • Maximum absence duration: 10 minutes
  • For extended absences (>10 min), request formal break approval
Time Off Requests
  • Submit all requests through SYCO Portal at least:
    • 2 weeks in advance for single days
    • 4 weeks for extended time off
  • After submission:
    • Email manager with detailed reason
    • Follow up within 48 hours if no response

Procedures

Important: All procedures must be documented in the customer case file before submission for approval.
Push to Cancel Process

Customer Agreement

Confirm customer verbally agrees to cancellation terms and elevated refund amount.

Documentation

Record all communication details in the case notes including:

  • Date/time of agreement
  • Refund amount approved
  • Customer confirmation method

System Update

Change status to "Pending Cancellation" and flag for manager review.

Settlement & Release (S&R)
Critical: Incorrect month placement in the S&R sheet will delay processing by 5-7 business days.

Agreement Finalization

Ensure all terms are confirmed with customer including:

  • Exact settlement amount
  • Effective cancellation date
  • Any special conditions

Sheet Placement

Submit request in Retentions | BM Department: S&R Request Sheet:

  • Current month tab for immediate processing
  • Future month tab for delayed settlements

Verification

Double-check:

  • Customer ID accuracy
  • Policy number
  • Settlement amount matches case notes

Account Pitches

Eligibility Assessment

Verify:

  • Claim relates to current active issue
  • Customer has no more than 2 prior claims
  • Policy is in good standing

Pitch Customization

Tailor approach based on:

  • Customer tenure
  • Claim history
  • Previous interactions

Documentation

Record all details in:

  • Customer profile notes
  • Special handling instructions
  • Follow-up calendar

Assistance Request Process

Initial Contact

Reach out to any available Tier 2 supervisor via:

  • Teams instant message
  • Direct phone extension
  • In-person if on-site

Response expectation: Within 15 minutes during normal hours

Escalation Criteria

Request manager escalation when:

  • Issue requires policy exception
  • Potential financial impact exceeds $500
  • Customer threatens legal action
  • Tier 2 cannot resolve within 30 minutes

Manager Escalation

For manager requests:

  • Complete Escalation Form in SYCO
  • Include all relevant case details
  • Attach supporting documentation
  • Flag as Urgent if time-sensitive

After Hours Support: For issues outside business hours, contact the on-call supervisor at 555-0100 (ext. 911 after 7pm).
