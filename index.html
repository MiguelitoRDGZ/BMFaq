Frequently Asked Questions and Procedures

Rules & Guidelines

Start of Shift

  • The first 1.5 to 2 hours of your shift should be dedicated to:
    • Checking your agenda
    • Reviewing emails
    • Responding to customer SMS

Personal Time Usage

  • You are allotted 15 minutes per day for personal time
  • If you exceed this limit, notify your Tier 2/3 or Manager

Leaving the Floor

  • If you need to step away for a personal call or any other reason, inform your Tier 2/3 or Manager

Time Off Requests

  • Submit time-off requests through SYCO
  • Notify your Manager with the reason for approval

Procedures

Push to Cancel

  • If the customer agrees to an elevated refund, submit the request and change the status to "Pending Cancellation"

Settlement & Release (S&R)

  • If the customer agrees to a partial settlement to cancel the policy:
    • Submit the request in the Retentions | BM Department: S&R Request Sheet
    • Ensure the request is placed in the correct section and month

Account Pitches

  • These apply only to the claim with the current issue (sometimes for the next claim)
  • Make the process easier and more lenient for the customer
