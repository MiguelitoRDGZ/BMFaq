Rules & Guidelines

Start of Shift

The first 1.5 to 2 hours of your shift is dedicated to checking your agenda, emails, and SMS that customers have sent to you.

Personal Time

You only get 15 minutes per day for personal time. If you exceed this limit, immediately notify your Tier 2/3 or Manager.

Leaving the Floor

Always communicate with your Tier 2/3 or Manager when leaving the floor for any reason so we're aware of your location if a customer calls.

Time Off Requests

Submit requests through SYCO at least 2 days in advance and notify your manager with the reason.

Cellphone Usage

Phone usage is not permitted on floor.

Calls Goal

Minimum of 15 calls per day. This is the baseline expectation for daily performance.

Vacation Requests

Submit through SYCO at least 2 weeks in advance. Approval may be denied during high season or for other valid reasons.

Procedures

Important: All procedures must be documented in the customer case file before submission for approval.

Push to Cancel

Customer Agreement

Confirm the customer agrees to the elevated refund and cancellation terms.

Documentation

Record all details of the agreement in the case notes.

System Update

Change the status to "Pending Cancellation" in the system.

Settlement & Release (S&R)

Open S&R Request Sheet
Critical: Placement in the wrong month/section will delay processing by 5-7 business days.

Settlement Agreement

Confirm the exact settlement amount and terms with the customer.

Sheet Submission

Submit request in the Retentions | BM Department: S&R Request sheet in the correct month section.

Verification

Double-check all customer and policy details before submission.

Account Pitches

Claim Assessment

Verify the pitch applies only to the current claim issue (or next claim if applicable).

Process Adjustment

Make the process easier and more lenient for the customer as appropriate.

Documentation

Record all special handling instructions for future reference.

Agenda Statuses

Open Agenda

Pending

Default status when initially reaching out to customer and no agreement has been reached yet.

Loss

Customer refuses to remove/update review. Cannot be used for BBB complaints without T2/3 or Manager approval.

Success

Customer agrees to update review but hasn't done it yet. Follow up required.

Removed

Review has been updated/removed. Note: If multiple reviews exist, all must be addressed before using this status.

Re-Evaluate Later

Used when claim is undergoing normal procedure or approach needs to be revised.

Unable To Reach (UTR)

After 5 unsuccessful contact attempts via phone, email, or SMS.
